About DuelZone

The browser gaming arena

DuelZone is the ultimate browser-based competitive gaming hub — engineered for two players, one screen, and zero compromise. Built on the belief that the best rivalries happen side by side, DuelZone brings friends, siblings, and rivals together through 30+ hand-crafted browser games that need nothing but a tab and a will to win.

Our Mission

We built DuelZone because gaming with a friend should be effortless. No installations, no accounts, no paywalls. Just two players, one keyboard or one shared screen, and instant competition. Our mission is to make every living room, classroom, and cafeteria a potential arena — accessible to anyone with a modern browser.

We pour love into every game, from pixel art assets to tightly-tuned gameplay mechanics, ensuring the experience feels polished and fair. We believe the best memories in gaming are made face-to-face, and DuelZone is our commitment to keeping local multiplayer alive in the browser era.

What We Offer

Our library spans every genre that thrives in competitive local play:

Strategy Reflex & Arcade Sports Simulation Word & Puzzle Card & Board

Every single game is built natively for two players sharing one device. We obsess over balanced gameplay — no unfair advantages, no pay-to-win, no dark patterns. Just pure, honest competition. All games feature responsive design, keyboard/touch controls, and are optimised for both desktop and mobile screens.

How It Works

DuelZone is entirely browser-based — no download, no sign-up, no subscription. Open the site, pick a game, and you're in. Player 1 and Player 2 each use their designated side of the keyboard (or touch zones on mobile). Every game clearly displays which keys each player uses, making it approachable even for complete newcomers.

Your saved games and recently played history are stored locally in your browser, so they're private to your device and persist across sessions. DuelZone also supports light ads to keep the service free for everyone — we keep them non-intrusive and respectful of your gaming experience.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

DuelZone is privacy-first. We do not sell or share your personal data.

Information We Collect

DuelZone does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share any personal details to play any game on the platform.

All game-related data — including your scores, saved games list, recently played history, and mute preferences — is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device, is never transmitted to our servers, and is fully under your control. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

Cookies & Local Storage

DuelZone itself does not plant any tracking, analytics, or session cookies. The only browser storage we use is localStorage for functional preferences: your sound/mute setting, your saved games, and your recently played list. These are purely operational and contain no personally identifiable information.

Please note: third-party ad networks we use (see Advertising section below) may set their own cookies subject to their respective privacy policies. You can manage or block these via your browser's cookie settings or ad-blocking tools.

Third-Party Services

DuelZone uses the following third-party services:

Google FontsLoads display typefaces (Orbitron, Rajdhani) via CDN. Does not receive user or gameplay data.
Ad NetworksWe display advertisements to keep DuelZone free. These networks may collect anonymised usage signals per their own policies.

We do not integrate analytics platforms (e.g., Google Analytics), social login SDKs, or any data brokers. DuelZone never sells your data to any third party.

Advertising

DuelZone displays advertisements to support free access to all games and to fund ongoing development. We are committed to keeping ads as non-intrusive as possible — they are displayed in designated zones and never interrupt active gameplay.

Our ad partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads that are relevant to your interests based on your browsing activity across other websites. This is standard industry practice called interest-based or behavioural advertising. If you prefer not to receive personalised ads, you can opt out via your browser settings, by using an ad-blocking extension, or through industry opt-out tools such as aboutads.info.

We do not control the content of third-party ads but we prohibit our ad partners from serving ads that are deceptive, malicious, or inappropriate for a general audience.

Children's Privacy

DuelZone is designed to be suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). Since we collect no personal information from any user, DuelZone is compliant with children's privacy regulations including COPPA.

Parents and guardians should be aware that our ad network partners may serve advertisements on the platform. If you have concerns about ad content your child is viewing, please contact us at [email protected] and we will investigate promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We review and update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make material changes, the "Last updated" date at the top of this document will be revised. We encourage you to revisit this policy occasionally.
